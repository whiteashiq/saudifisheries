saudi fisheries company

Saudi Fisheries Company was established by Royal Decree No. M / 7 in 1980 as a Saudi joint stock company in the field of fishing mainly. The company’s purpose base was expanded to include the investment of living aquatic resources and related industries and marketing them internally and externally. The remaining for individual investors is 60%.
